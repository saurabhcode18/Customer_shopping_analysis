# 📊 Customer Shopping Behavior Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

This project presents a comprehensive Customer Shopping Behavior Analytics Dashboard developed using Power BI. The dashboard analyzes customer purchasing patterns, product performance, sales trends, and customer loyalty metrics to support data-driven business decisions.

## Project Objective

The primary objective of this project is to identify key revenue drivers, understand customer behavior, evaluate loyalty programs, and uncover actionable business insights through interactive visualizations.

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX
* SQL
* Python (Exploratory Data Analysis)
* CSV Dataset

## Dashboard Pages

### Page 1: Sales Overview

* Total Revenue Analysis
* Total Customers
* Average Order Value
* Average Customer Rating
* Revenue by Category
* Top Products by Revenue
* Revenue by Season
* Revenue by Gender
* Average Purchase Amount by Size

### Page 2: Customer & Loyalty Analytics

* Subscriber Percentage
* Discount Usage Analysis
* Revenue by Age Group
* Purchase Frequency Analysis
* Subscription Revenue Analysis
* Payment Method Analysis
* Discount Impact on Revenue

## Key Insights

* Clothing generated the highest revenue among all product categories.
* Male customers contributed approximately 68% of total revenue.
* Young Adults were the highest spending customer segment.
* Sales remained relatively stable across all seasons.
* Revenue was distributed across multiple products, reducing dependency on a single item.
* Non-subscribers contributed the majority of revenue.
* Payment method preferences were evenly distributed among customers.

## KPIs Tracked

* Total Revenue
* Total Customers
* Average Order Value
* Average Rating
* Subscriber Percentage
* Discount Usage Percentage
* Average Previous Purchases
* Average Purchase Days

## Business Value

The dashboard helps stakeholders:

* Monitor sales performance
* Identify high-value customer segments
* Understand customer purchasing behavior
* Evaluate marketing and discount strategies
* Improve customer retention initiatives

## Project Outcome

Developed an interactive business intelligence solution that transforms raw customer transaction data into meaningful insights, enabling informed decision-making and strategic planning.
